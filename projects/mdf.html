Rice Straw based MDF (Board)

Abstract

The objective of this project was to explore an eco-friendly and sustainable alternative to traditional wood-based MDF. By utilizing rice straw fibers, which are a byproduct of the rice industry and often discarded or burned, the research aimed to develop a resource-efficient and cost-effective method for producing MDF. This investigation seeked to assess the feasibility of rice straw-based MDF in terms of its mechanical properties, environmental impact, and potential for commercial application, thus contributing to the development of sustainable and renewable materials in the construction and furniture industries.
